# 🚀 AI Girlfriend - Professional Publishing Guide

This guide provides comprehensive, step-by-step instructions for uploading and publishing the **AI Girlfriend** application on **APKPure**.

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

Before you begin the submission process, ensure you have the following ready:

- [ ] **Developer Account**: Registered and verified at [APKPure Developer Console](https://developer.apkpure.com/).
- [ ] **Production Build**: A signed `APK` or `AAB` (Android App Bundle).
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: A public URL for your application's privacy policy (e.g., hosted on GitHub Pages or your official website).
- [ ] **Visual Assets**: Professional-grade screenshots, icons, and banners (see [Asset Requirements](#-asset-requirements)).

---

## 📂 Required Files

### 1. Application Package
- **Format**: `.apk` or `.aab`
- **Max Size**: 2 GB (if your app exceeds this, consider using XAPK format).
- **Signing**: Ensure the app is signed with your production keystore.

### 2. Asset Requirements
| Asset type | Recommended Size | Format | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **App Icon** | 512 x 512 px | PNG/JPG | High-resolution logo of the app. |
| **Screenshots** | 480 x 800 px (min) | PNG/JPG | At least 3–5 representative UI screenshots. |
| **Feature Graphic** | 1024 x 500 px | PNG/JPG | Promotional banner for store highlights. |

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Submission Workflow

### Step 1: Account Setup
1. Visit the [APKPure Developer Console](https://developer.apkpure.com/).
2. Create an account or sign in with your existing credentials.
3. Complete the developer profile (if not already done) by providing necessary business/developer information.

### Step 2: Initiate App Submission
1. In the dashboard, click on the **"Add Application"** button.
2. Select the **Category** (e.g., "AI Lifestyle", "Entertainment").
3. Enter the **Application Name** and **Package Name** (e.g., `com.aigirlfriend.companion`).

### Step 3: Fill in App Details
Provide a compelling description to attract users and satisfy review criteria:
- **Short Description**: A catchy one-liner (max 80 chars).
- **Full Description**: Detailed features, user benefits, and clear value proposition.
- **What's New**: Release notes for the current version.

### Step 4: Upload Assets & Package
1. Upload the high-res icon and screenshots in the **Media** section.
2. Upload the `APK/AAB` file under the **Manage Versions** tab.
3. Provide the **Privacy Policy URL** (Mandatory).

### Step 5: Content Rating & Pricing
1. Select the appropriate **Content Rating** (ensure it matches the AI companion nature).
2. Set the **Price** (most apps are Free on APKPure).

## ✅ Tips for Successful Approval

> [!TIP]
> APKPure reviews are manual. Follow these tips to ensure a smooth approval process:

- **Consistent Branding**: Ensure the app name in the console matches the name inside the APK.
- **Privacy First**: AI companion apps must have a clear privacy policy regarding chat data.
- **Functional Stability**: Test your production build on multiple devices before uploading to avoid "crash-on-launch" rejections.
- **High-Quality Context**: Use professional feature graphics that clearly represent the AI interaction.
- **Avoid Copyright Infringement**: Do not use trademarked images or characters that you do not own the rights to.

---

## ⏳ Review Process
Once submitted, the manual review typically takes **3 to 7 business days**. You will receive an email notification once your app is approved or if additional information is required.
